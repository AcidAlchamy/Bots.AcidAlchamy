Translator Bot

About Translator Bot

Translator Bot is a multi-functional tool designed to bridge language barriers within Discord communities. Its real-time translation capabilities provide seamless communication across different languages.

Features

  • Real-Time Translation: Automatically translates foreign languages to your language of choice.
  • Customizable Settings: Use the !setlang command to customize translation settings for channels, roles, and individual users.
